Privacy Policy for THE 4TH DIMENSION

Last Updated: March 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application "THE 4TH DIMENSION". Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1. Introduction

THE 4TH DIMENSION Development Team ("we," "our," or "us") respects the privacy of our users ("user" or "you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application THE 4TH DIMENSION. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Collection of Your Information

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the Application includes:

2.1 Personal Data

When you use our app, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication information.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data to enable core app functionality, specifically for generating quantum points within your vicinity. Location data is only collected when the app is in use.
  • User Content: We collect any notes, intentions, and manifestations you create within the app.
  • Device Information: We collect information about your mobile device including device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our Application, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Information: Details about how you use the app, including time spent, features used, and actions taken
  • Log Data: Information that your browser or device sends whenever you use our app, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data
  • App Errors: Crash data and error messages to help us improve the application

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable and provide the core functionality of the app, including quantum point generation which requires location data
  • Process and fulfill in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Store and process your notes, intentions, and other content you create
  • Improve and optimize the application's performance, usability, and effectiveness
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Communicate with you about updates, changes, or new features
  • Protect the security and integrity of our app
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Firebase Integration and Data Storage

Our app uses Google Firebase services to store and manage user data:

  • Firebase Authentication: Used to manage user accounts and authentication
  • Firestore Database: Stores user data, including your notes, intentions, manifestations, token balance, and subscription information
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to help us improve the app
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports to help us diagnose and fix technical issues

All data stored in Firebase is protected according to Google's security practices. For more information about how Google handles your data, please refer to Firebase's Privacy Policy.

5. Google Maps API

Our app uses the Google Maps API to display maps and enable location-based features. When you use these features, Google may collect data about your location and how you use the Maps service, as described in the Google Privacy Policy.

The app uses location data to:

  • Display your current position on the map
  • Generate quantum points within your vicinity
  • Allow you to save and interact with locations on the map

You can disable location permissions at any time through your device settings, but this will limit core functionality of the app.

6. In-App Purchases and Payments

THE 4TH DIMENSION app offers in-app purchases for token packs and subscriptions. When you make a purchase:

  • The payment transaction is processed by Google Play (for Android) or Apple App Store (for iOS)
  • We receive and store a purchase record (transaction ID, product ID, purchase date) but do not have access to your payment details such as credit card information
  • Your purchase history is stored in your user account to manage your tokens and subscription status
  • For subscriptions, we maintain records of your subscription status, tier, and renewal dates

For more information about how Google or Apple process payments, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

7. Advertising

The app contains optional rewarded advertisements provided through Google AdMob. When you choose to view an ad:

  • AdMob may collect device information, location data, and identifiers to serve personalized ads
  • We track the number of ads you've watched to manage daily limits and token rewards
  • You can choose not to watch ads without impacting the core functionality of the app

For more information about how Google AdMob handles your data, please refer to Google's Advertising Policy.

8. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations. Your information may be disclosed as follows:

8.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party vendors, service providers, contractors, or agents who perform services for us or on our behalf and require access to such information to do that work. These third parties are limited to:

  • Firebase (for authentication, data storage, analytics, and crash reporting)
  • Google Maps API (for location-based features)
  • Google AdMob (for serving in-app advertisements)
  • Google Play and Apple App Store (for processing in-app purchases)

8.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information where we are legally required to do so in order to comply with applicable law, governmental requests, a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process, such as in response to a court order or a subpoena.

8.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our Application of any change in ownership or uses of your information.

9. User Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal data we hold
  • Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data
  • Data Export: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction: You can request that we restrict the processing of your data under certain circumstances
  • Objection: You can object to our processing of your data based on our legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

10. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

Specific security measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication procedures
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel only

11. Policy for Children

We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If you become aware of any data we have collected from children under age 13, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

THE 4TH DIMENSION app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

12. Your Choices

You can control your information in the following ways:

  • Account Information: You can review and change your account information by logging into your account settings
  • Location Data: You can enable or disable location permissions through your device settings
  • Storage: You can delete your content (notes, intentions, etc.) directly within the app
  • Communications: You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions
  • Device Permissions: You can modify app permissions through your device settings
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account through the app settings or via our web form (see Account Deletion section below)

13. Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your THE 4TH DIMENSION account and associated personal data. There are two ways to delete your account:

  1. In-App Deletion: Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Delete Account". You will need to confirm your password to complete this process.
  2. Web Form Request: If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion through our web form at: delete-account.html

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove all your personal information, including:

  • Profile details
  • Saved intentions and manifestations
  • Journal entries and notes
  • Quantum points and saved locations
  • Token balance and purchase history
  • Subscription information

Account deletion requests submitted through the web form will be processed within 14 business days. Once your account is deleted, the action cannot be undone, and you will lose access to all your data.

14. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

For significant changes, we will provide a more prominent notice, which may include an in-app notification or email notification to the email address associated with your account.
